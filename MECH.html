Mechanical Engineering

Since its inception in 1980, the Department of Mechanical Engineering has been a cornerstone of our institute, fostering innovation and excellence in the field. With a team of highly qualified faculty mentors holding PhDs, the department is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of engineers through cutting-edge blended learning techniques. Our comprehensive curriculum covers essential aspects of mechanical engineering, preparing students to design, build, and optimise complex systems and machinery. Join us to transform your passion into practical solutions and drive technological advancements in the mechanical engineering world.
