About GITAM

GITAM Deemed to be University (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) is a private deemed university founded in 1980 in Visakhapatnam, inspired by Gandhi's ideals, now with campuses in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Visakhapatnam, offering multidisciplinary education in Engineering, Management, Sciences, Law, Pharmacy, and Medicine, focusing on research, innovation, and industry-relevant skills. It's recognized for its strong faculty, significant research output, and placements, with NAAC A++ accreditation and a global alumni network.
